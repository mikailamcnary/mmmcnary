Lifestyle

I'm Mikaila, and it is fair to say that I among many other millenials live a broke college student, minimalist lifestyle. I am passionate about sustainability, animals and am vegetarian. And not to mention, I'm a cat mom to my best friend Kitty.

When I am not studying or working, some of my hobbies include running, yoga, hiking, drawing, traveling, cooking new recipes and watching too much netflix.

This upcoming year, my goals include traveling more, doing more yoga and hopefully training to run a half marathon! I plan on dedicating this site to share my experiences.
