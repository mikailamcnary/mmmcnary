About

Hello, my name is Mikaila McNary and I am a third year undergraduate student at Cal Poly Orfalea College of Business. I am studying Economics with a concentration in Quantitative Analysis as well as a minoring in Political Science. I'm interested in exploring how various issues in our political economy interact with and affect the overall macro economy.

Within my short three years at Cal Poly, I have assisted the Economics department in conducting research regarding the social and economic impacts of legalized cannabis in certain states, in addition to its potential prenatal affects. I have also written a paper in collaboration with professors from both the Economics and Political Science departments analyzing the social costs and micro economic impacts of targeted regulation of abortion provider (TRAP) laws.

In addition to my studies, I have immersed myself in campus life by being an active member of both Cal Poly Economics Society as well as Pilipino Culture Exchange for all three years. I hope to continue my involvement in these clubs, as they are an integral part of my identity as both an Economics student and Filipina American.
